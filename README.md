BCG Data for Decision Makers – Marketing Analytics Simulation
# **Overview**

This project was completed as part of the Data for Decision Makers Job Simulation by Boston Consulting Group on the Forage.

The simulation focused on analyzing digital marketing campaign data for a hypothetical consumer goods company. The goal was to evaluate campaign performance across multiple marketing channels and provide data-driven recommendations to improve marketing effectiveness.

## **Business Problem**

A consumer goods company wanted to understand:

Which marketing channels were driving the highest conversions

How different customer audiences responded to campaigns

What strategies could improve overall campaign performance

The task was to analyze campaign data and present insights that would help guide future marketing decisions.

### **Project Tasks**
1. Campaign Performance Analysis

Reviewed campaign data across multiple channels

Examined customer conversion trends

Compared effectiveness of different marketing approaches

2. Customer Behavior Insights

Interpreted customer interaction data

Identified patterns in audience engagement

Evaluated which audiences converted the most

3. Business Recommendation

Synthesized insights from the analysis

Developed a clear recommendation for improving campaign strategy

Presented findings in a concise, business-focused format

### **Tools Used**

Microsoft Excel

Data interpretation and analysis techniques

Business communication and insight presentation

Key Skills Demonstrated

Marketing analytics

Data interpretation

Customer behavior analysis

Business insight generation

Communicating data-driven recommendations

## **Key Outcome**

The analysis helped identify which marketing channels and audiences were most effective, allowing the company to better allocate resources and optimize future campaigns.

## **About the Simulation**

The program provided practical exposure to the type of analytical work performed by consultants and marketing analytics teams.

It involved working with simulated business data to solve a real-world style marketing problem and delivering recommendations aligned with the client’s goals.
